## July 16: AI Breakthrough Club Roadshow at Xishan OPC Youth Innovation Space

On the day of the official launch of the OPC Youth Innovation Space in Huayi Community, Xishan District, Xiao An Lao Si led the AI Breakthrough Club team to report on the club and the Zhitu Yunling project in front of leaders including the Deputy Secretary of the District Committee and the Director of the District Organization Department.

### Core Content of the Roadshow
- **Project Progress**: The AI Breakthrough Club has held multiple events, helping participants go from zero foundation to independently building AI applications.
- **Future Plans**: Rooted in Xishan District, providing AI training and technical support for the OPC space.

### Q&A Session
Q: How can AI serve local small and medium-sized enterprises?
A: We have developed a set of low-cost automated workflow tools that reduce labor costs by over 30%.

Q: What successful cases does the club have?
A: We have helped more than 10 teams implement AI applications, covering areas such as customer service and marketing.

### Summary
This roadshow was not just a report but also a practice of the concept of AI for all. Xiao An Lao Si believes that the next step for AI is to move into communities and industries.

AI is not a distant future but a tool of the present. Seize it, and you too can create value.
